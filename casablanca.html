Casablanca (1942)

Director: Michael Curtiz

Cast: Humphrey Bogart, Ingrid Bergman, Paul Henreid

Description: "Casablanca" is a timeless classic set during World War II. It tells the story of Rick Blaine, a nightclub owner in Casablanca, who must choose between his love for Ilsa and helping her husband, a resistance leader, escape from the Nazis. The film is renowned for its memorable dialogue, iconic performances, and haunting soundtrack.

Release Year: 1942
